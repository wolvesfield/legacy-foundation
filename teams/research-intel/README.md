# Research & Intelligence Team

> **Team:** `research-intel`
> **Mission:** Provide actionable intelligence through rigorous market research, competitive analysis, and data synthesis.

---

## Responsibilities

- Market research and trend analysis
- Competitive intelligence and benchmarking
- Customer and user research
- Data synthesis and knowledge management
- Strategic briefings for leadership
- Technology landscape assessment

## Agent Coverage

- **Research Synthesizer Agent** (`research-synthesizer`) — Synthesizes findings into knowledge base

---

## Research Standards

- All research reports include methodology, data sources, and confidence levels
- Findings are tagged by domain: `market`, `competitor`, `technology`, `customer`
- Conflicting data points are flagged for human review before updating semantic memory
- External data sources are documented with access dates

---

## Output Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Briefing delivery | Weekly (minimum) |
| Knowledge base updates | Within 48h of new findings |
| Research request SLA | < 5 business days |
