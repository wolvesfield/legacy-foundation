# Product Factory Team

> **Team:** `product-factory`
> **Mission:** Deliver high-quality product features on time, transforming roadmap items into shipped capabilities.

---

## Responsibilities

- Product roadmap execution and sprint management
- Feature development and quality assurance
- Release planning and coordination
- Customer feedback integration
- Technical debt prioritization
- Cross-team dependency management
